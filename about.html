Our Story

From a Commission
to a Global Embrace

Love Embrace Global Outreach traces its origins to Spreading Jesus the Christ Commission, a non-denominational ministry founded on August 9, 2016, under the Lordship of Jesus Christ of Nazareth — with a clear and divinely inspired mandate to proclaim the Gospel and raise disciples across all nations.

Under the spiritual oversight of Pastor Isaac Baffour Senkyire, the ministry grew from local outreach to a vibrant movement reaching schools, health facilities, prisons, commercial spaces, social media platforms, and international missions beyond Ghana.

On May 5, 2026, the ministry was officially rebranded as Love Embrace Global Outreach, reflecting an expanded vision and deeper commitment to demonstrating and sharing God's love on a global scale.

Our Journey
August 2016

Spreading Jesus the Christ Commission Founded

Ministry established with a mandate to proclaim the Gospel, rooted in Mark 16:15–18 and Matthew 28:19–20.

October 2018

Loose Them & Bring Them Launched

Divinely inspired SHS outreach launched at Kwasi Oppong SHS, Kumasi, growing to reach ~100,000 students.

March 2019

First Jesus is Lord Crusade

Inaugural crusade held at Abetifi-Kwahu, Eastern Region — the start of 8 community crusades across Ghana.

July 2020

Global Fasting & Prayers Begins

Weekly Prayergelism sessions launched via Zoom, mobilizing believers globally to intercede for souls.

April 2023

Soteria Conference Launched

Virtual soul-winning platform inaugurated from Denver, Colorado, reaching a global audience.

February 2026

North Gonja Outreach — "Love Embrace"

15 communities reached; 6,286 people impacted; 699 souls saved in the North Gonja District.

May 2026

Rebranded: Love Embrace Global Outreach

Official transformation reflecting renewed vision and deeper global mission.

Meet the Labourers

Pastor Isaac Baffour Senkyire
Chief Labourer · Founder

A divinely commissioned end-time apostle with a comprehensive ministry mandate encompassing evangelism, teaching, the prophetic, and healing. Widely recognized as a servant of God marked by strong faith and the manifestation of signs, wonders, and miracles.

A passionate soul-winner who has ministered in the USA, Canada, UK, Australia, UAE, China, South Africa, and across Africa. He serves as a Lecturer in Computer Science at Ghana Communication Technology University (GCTU).

Lady Joyce Koryo Baffour Senkyire
Partner in Ministry

A devoted follower and servant of Jesus Christ, deeply passionate about the salvation and transformation of souls. Faithful in ministry from childhood, she has participated in numerous rural crusades and served as a committed intercessor.

Lady Joyce ministers a message centered on purity, kingdom service, prayer, faith, commitment, hope, and salvation. Professionally, she is a trained Food Technologist serving as Senior Regulatory Officer at Ghana's Food and Drugs Authority (FDA).
